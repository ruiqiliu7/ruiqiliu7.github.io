About Me

"A good computer scientist is like a good detective... first, they seek to understand the problem at hand, then they try to map the clues given in the problem to patterns that they have developed mentally/internally. The only way to become a good detective is to solve a lot of cases/crimes. Similarly, to become a good computer scientist one must solve a lot of problems & learn from a lot of mistakes. ‚Äù

In the process of programming, I did experience the joy of solving mysteries like a detective and learned to grow from mistakes. This has also become one of the motivations for me to apply for postgraduate studies in computer-related majors and continue my studies.
